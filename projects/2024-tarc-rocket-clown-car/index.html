2024 TARC Rocket - Clown Car

The American Rocketry Challenge (TARC) ★ Aug 2023 - May 2024

Clown Car is the rocket my team built for the 2024 season of The American Rocketry Challenge (TARC). It achieved a qualifying score on one of two flights required for national competition.

Skills & Tools: OpenRocket CAD, Flight Simulation, Autodesk Fusion, Prototyping/Iteration, Communication, Team Leadership

Objectives/Features:

  • Can carry a payload (large egg) to exactly 820 feet and descend safely to the ground in the span of 43 to 46 seconds
  • Adaptable to a multitude of flight conditions due to variable mass system and custom fin designs

Takeaways:

  • Ensured effective collaboration as team leader throughout the prototyping process and during fast-paced launch/test days
  • Gained expertise in OpenRocket CAD and simulation software
  • Learned how to set up and utilize Glowforge laser cutter, CNC router, and 3D printer
