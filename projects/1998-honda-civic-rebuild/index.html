1998 Salvage Title Honda Civic Rebuild

Personal Project ★ May 2021 - Oct 2022

I rebuilt a $600 salvage title 1998 Honda Civic that would've ended up in a junkyard otherwise. This project spanned two summers and helped me develop several skills.

Skills & Tools: OBDII/CAN Diagnostics, Automotive Electronics, Exhaust/Brake/Suspension Repair, Budgeting

Objectives/Features:

  • Replaced front-end components including wheel bearings, CV axles, and brakes
  • Overhauled exhaust system due to extensive rust damage
  • Restored interior by stripping and reinstalling each piece after treatment
  • Prepared car for official state inspection - passed and obtained rebuilt title

Takeaways:

  • Learned how to develop a budget to reduce costs for parts, employing alternative sources like scrapyards
  • Learned from online and offline resources and asked mentors for advice, along with utilizing problem-solving skills to overcome obstacles
  • Fostered local community by gathering people interested in automotive to learn about aspects of engineering through hands-on demonstrations
  • Shared project with online audience through personal blog posts
